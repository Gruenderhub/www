About the Gründerhub FrankfurtRheinMain

Gründerhub FrankfurtRheinMain is an initiative by private, commercial and communal entities to support innovative founders.

About the Founders Table FrankfurtRheinMain

Founders Table FrankfurtRheinMain is a meeting by founders for founders and happens on the third Monday of every month. It is the perfect place to connect with other founders from our region in a relaxed atmosphere without having to endure yet another sales pitch. For the evening we are the exclusive guests of Circus where you can enjoy a great buffet prepared by its fantastic chef.
